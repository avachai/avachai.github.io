Can I compress AvaChai avatars?

Depending on what you need AvaChai for, you might not even need to compress avatars. The service has a built-in downloader, which supports PNG and SVG file types. Now, if you need a good-quality avatar for your HTML website, an SVG-format avatar will do. SVG avatars are up to 80% smaller than PNG avatars. This means SVG is perfect for your website, but PNG can be up to 4 times larger. However, social media services (like Instagram, TikTok, and Facebook) only accept PNG/JPG/ICO file formats. In this case, you can compress AvaChai avatars, but they will lose quality.

Services such as TinyPNG and CompressPNG are not recommended and shouldn't be used.

Since this is a growing question, AvaChai is working with several other service providers to create a safe compressing solution.
